Getting here

By road

Handleigh Hall Estate (use postcode OX32 3JF) Approach from the A40, follow the road sign to Coley. The Coachman car park is approximately half a mile along the road, signed to the right. For Coley village turn right at the cross roads approximately 1 mile along the road. Parking: For Handleigh Estate: Coachman car park 163:SP158143, Duck Pond 163:SP175154 (off A40 towards Coley). Both car parks are pay and display. SatNav: If using SatNav, use postcode OX32 3JF for the Coachman car park. For West Barn car park, use postcode OX32 9NA.

By public transport

For information about travelling to this site by public transport please visit the Cotswold AONB 'Explore the Cotswolds' website.

Opening times

Handleigh Hall is open from 10:00 until 17:00 every day except for New Year's Day, Easter Sunday, Christmas Day and New Year's Eve.
